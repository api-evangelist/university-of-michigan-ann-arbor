The University of Michigan Library is collaborating with the Rackham Graduate School to make 150 years of graduate research publicly accessible. According to Charles Watkinson, director of the University of Michigan Press, the initiative creates a "dynamic, accessible, and measurable global resource" that demonstrates how U-M dissertations serve as powerful vehicles for worldwide scholarly impact.
